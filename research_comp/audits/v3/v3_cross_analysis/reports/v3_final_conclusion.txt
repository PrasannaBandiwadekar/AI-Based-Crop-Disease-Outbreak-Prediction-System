=== V3 Final Conclusion ===

1. Most contributing groups: Rolling and Risk features capture cumulative weather effects better than raw point data.
2. Engineered features provide significant signal over raw variables, but many lags (1, 2, 3) are redundant with rolling means.
3. V3 is slightly over-engineered; redundancy analysis shows many features with >0.95 correlation.
4. Production set: Recommend a 'V3-Light' set excluding redundant lags and highly unstable risk indices.
